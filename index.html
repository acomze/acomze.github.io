Biography

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University, supervised by Prof. Xu Chen. Before that, I received my B.E. degree with honor at School of Data and Computer Science, Sun Yat-sen University in June 2018.

Research Interests

My research interest lies in building edge intelligence with real-time responsiveness, systematic resource efficiency, and theoretical performance guarantee. Currently, I focus on attaining expedited, cost-effective GNN model serving with distributed edge computing.
  • Applications and Services
    • Edge-assisted autonomous drone navigation with neural adaptive scheduler [AdaDrone, ICDCS '22]
    • Multi-robot SLAM in cloud-Edge-robot contimum [RecSLAM, IoTJ '22]
    • Edge-cloud synergistic inference [Edgent, TWC' 20] [Boomerang, NET '20]
  • Systems and Infrastructure
    • GNN model serving with distributed fog servers [Fograph, WWW '22]
    • Cooperative inference over heterogeneous edge devices [CoEdge, ToN '20]
  • Theory and Modeling Frameworks
    • Review and categorization of edge intelligence [Survey, PIEEE '19]
    • Edge resource probing and exploitation [CERP, RTSS '19]
    • Joint DNN partitioning and resource allocation [IAO, IoTJ '20]
