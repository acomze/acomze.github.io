Biography

I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University, supervised by Prof. Xu Chen. Before that, I received my B.E. degree with honor at School of Data and Computer Science, Sun Yat-sen University.

Research Interests

My research interest lies in building edge intelligence systems with real-time responsiveness, systematic resource efficiency, and theoretical performance guarantee. Currently, I focus on developing scalable GNN model serving systems with distributed edge computing.

Research overview:

  • Systems and Infrastructure
    • GNN model serving with distributed fog servers: Fograph [WWW '22]
    • Collaborative training/inference over heterogeneous edge devices: Eco-FL [ICPP '22], CoEdge [TON '21]
    • Edge-cloud synergistic progressive inference: Edgent [TWC '20], Boomerang [NET '19]
  • Applications and Services
    • Edge-assisted adaptive autonomous drone navigation: AdaDrone [ICDCS '22]
    • Multi-robot SLAM in robot-edge-cloud continuum: RecSLAM [IoTJ '22]
    • Progressive image enhancement for mobile vision: Apie [MSN '22]
  • Theory and Modeling
    • Graph data layout optimization for distributed GNN processing: GLAD [JSAC '22]
    • Online edge resource exploration and exploitation: CERP [RTSS '19]
    • Definition and taxonomy of edge intelligence: Review [PIEEE '19]
