Biography

I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University, supervised by Prof. Xu Chen. Before that, I received my B.E. degree with honor at School of Data and Computer Science, Sun Yat-sen University in June 2018.

Research Interests

My research interest lies in building edge intelligence systems with real-time responsiveness, systematic resource efficiency, and theoretical performance guarantee. Currently, I focus on developing expedited, cost-effective GNN model serving with distributed edge computing.
  • Applications and Services
    • Edge-assisted adptive autonomous drone navigation [AdaDrone, ICDCS '22]
    • Multi-robot SLAM in robot-edge-cloud continuum [RecSLAM, IoTJ '22]
    • Edge-cloud synergistic progressive inference [Edgent, TWC '20] [Boomerang, NET '19]
  • Systems and Infrastructure
    • GNN model serving with distributed fog servers [Fograph, WWW '22]
    • Collaborative edge training via pipeline model parallelism [Eco-FL, ICPP '22]
    • Collaborative edge inference via partitioned model parallelism [CoEdge, ToN '21]
  • Theory and Modeling
    • Review and categorization of edge intelligence [Survey, PIEEE '19]
    • Online edge resource exploration and exploitation [CERP, RTSS '19]
    • Joint optimization on DNN partitioning and resource allocation [IAO, IoTJ '21]
